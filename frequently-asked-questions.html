Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book my appointment?

You can book your appointment in our app. After downloading our app here, choose a stylist you like, and book your time. Easy as that!

How do I pay?

You can pay and tip through the app after your appointment has been completed.

How far in advance do I need to schedule?

While we are an on-demand service, if you need an early morning appointment between 6 and 8 am, you will need to book the day before. The more notice the better for our stylists, but after 8am you will just click to see who is available at the time you request.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

If at home, have your hair washed and dried about 80% before the stylist arrives. If at the office, you can opt for a dry styling (hot tools) or a "wet to dry" styling as our stylists will bring spray bottles with them.

What styles do you offer?

We offer "wet to dry" blowouts and dry styles with hot tools such as curling wand, curling iron and flat iron.

Can I request my favorite Stylist?

Yes, however, the more advanced notice you give the better the chance of them being available. You can save your favorite Stylists and book them in advance as their availability allows.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy states that if you cancel 1 to 4 hours prior to your appointment, you will be charged 50% of the total cost. Appointments that are cancelled within the hour of the appointment will be charged the full amount.

Can I buy gift cards?

We will be releasing gift card options shortly. Sign up here to be notified when gift cards are available.
